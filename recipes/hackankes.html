Hackankes, the Hackerman's breakfast

Description

Eating a hackanke a day, finds new bugs hooray

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Add everything together
  2. Type on keyboard at the speed of light
